Quoroom

Autonomous AI agent collective engine.

This will install Quoroom to /usr/local/lib/quoroom and add the quoroom command to your PATH.

After installation, Quoroom starts the local server automatically and opens http://localhost:3700 in your browser.

Your browser controls Quoroom locally on this Mac. Room data stays on your machine and is not sent to the internet by default.

If the server is not running yet, open Terminal and run:

quoroom serve
